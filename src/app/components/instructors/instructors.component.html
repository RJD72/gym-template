Instructors

Tigris "Stripes" Morales

Tigris "Stripes" Morales is the head instructor at Valor Jiu-Jitsu Academy. With a fierce yet graceful approach, Tigris has mastered Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, earning accolades in numerous international competitions, including the Wild Grappling Championships. Renowned for his agility, strength, and precision, Tigris teaches with an emphasis on balance, technique, and mental fortitude. His students admire his ability to seamlessly blend power with finesse, making him a revered figure in the BJJ community. Tigris is dedicated to cultivating discipline and respect in his students, ensuring they excel both on and off the mats.

Rhino "The Guard" Jenkins

Rhino "The Guard" Jenkins is a highly skilled Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu instructor and a formidable presence on the mats. Standing at an impressive height and possessing immense strength, Rhino has adapted his natural attributes to excel in the sport. Known for his unbreakable guard and powerful submissions, he has won several BJJ tournaments, including the prestigious Animal BJJ Championships. Rhinos teaching approach combines rigorous physical training with a focus on technique and strategy, making him a beloved coach at the Safari Grappling Academy. His dedication to the art and his unique perspective inspire both human and animal students alike.

Goro "The Grip" Strong

Goro "The Grip" Strong is a renowned Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu instructor at Valor BJJ Academy. Known for his incredible strength and unparalleled grip, Goro has dominated the BJJ scene, winning multiple titles in the Primate Grappling Championships. His teaching style focuses on leveraging natural strength and developing impeccable technique, making him a favorite among students. Goro emphasizes the importance of resilience and strategic thinking, inspiring his students to push their limits. His dedication to the art of BJJ and his unique approach to training have earned him a respected place in the martial arts community.

Felina "Shadow" Martinez

Felina "Shadow" Martinez is a distinguished Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu instructor at Valor BJJ Academy. With her swift and elusive style, Felina has earned multiple titles, including the Women's World BJJ Championship. Known for her agility, precision, and tactical brilliance, she is both a fierce competitor and an inspiring coach. Felina's teaching philosophy centers on empowering her students, focusing on technique, mental toughness, and adaptability. Her ability to connect with students and her dedication to their progress make her a beloved and respected figure in the BJJ community.
