Kids / Teens

Tiny Grapplers (Ages 3-5)

Welcome to our Tiny Grapplers program, designed specifically for our youngest BJJ enthusiasts! At this age, children are full of energy and curiosity, and our classes aim to channel that enthusiasm into fun and educational activities. Through playful drills and basic Jiu-Jitsu movements, your child will develop important motor skills, balance, and coordination. Our Tiny Grapplers program is all about creating a positive first experience with martial arts, fostering a love for physical activity, and building a foundation for future success in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

Little Warriors (Ages 6-8)

Our Little Warriors program is tailored for children ages 6 to 8, a critical period for developing both physical and mental skills. In these classes, we focus on fundamental BJJ techniques, instilling discipline, respect, and teamwork. Your child will learn self-defense skills while gaining confidence and improving their fitness. Our experienced instructors create a supportive and engaging environment where kids can thrive, make friends, and enjoy the process of learning. The Little Warriors program is perfect for children ready to take their first steps toward mastery in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

Junior Champions (Ages 9-15)

The Junior Champions program is designed for our older kids, ages 9 to 15, who are ready to dive deeper into the art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. This program emphasizes more advanced techniques, strategy, and sparring in a safe and controlled setting. As your child progresses, they will gain a deeper understanding of BJJ, enhancing their problem-solving abilities, resilience, and sportsmanship. Our goal is to equip our Junior Champions with the skills and mindset needed to succeed both on and off the mats. Whether your child aims to compete or simply enjoy the benefits of BJJ, this program will challenge and inspire them to reach their full potential.
