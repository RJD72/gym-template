White Belt Fundamentals

Welcome to the White Belt Fundamentals program at Valor Jiu-Jitsu Academy. This class is specifically designed for beginners who are new to Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. We understand that starting something new can be intimidating, which is why we focus on creating a welcoming and supportive environment. In this program, you'll learn the essential techniques and concepts that form the foundation of BJJ. Our experienced instructors will guide you through basic positions, escapes, and submissions, ensuring you develop a solid understanding and confidence on the mats. Join us and take the first step on your BJJ journey with the support of a community that’s dedicated to your growth and success.

No Gi/Submission Wrestling

Our No Gi/Submission Wrestling program is perfect for those looking to enhance their grappling skills without the traditional gi. This dynamic class focuses on the principles of submission wrestling, emphasizing speed, agility, and technique. Training without the gi allows for a different style of grappling that can be both challenging and exhilarating. You'll learn a variety of submissions, takedowns, and positional strategies that are applicable in both sport and self-defense scenarios. Whether you’re new to No Gi or looking to sharpen your existing skills, this class offers an exciting and practical approach to grappling.

Gi Classes

In our Gi Classes, we delve into the rich traditions and techniques of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu with the gi. Training with the gi allows for a deeper exploration of grips, control, and technical precision. These classes cover a wide range of techniques, from fundamental movements to advanced strategies, suitable for all skill levels. You'll learn how to effectively use the gi to execute submissions, escapes, and sweeps. Our Gi Classes are designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of BJJ, enhancing your ability to control and submit opponents while building a strong foundation of discipline and respect.

Competition Classes

For those looking to take their training to the next level, our Competition Classes offer an intense and focused environment to prepare for tournaments. This program is tailored for individuals who are serious about competing and improving their competitive edge. You'll train under the guidance of our experienced coaching staff, who bring a wealth of knowledge from their own competitive successes. These classes emphasize advanced techniques, conditioning, strategy, and mental preparation, ensuring you're ready to perform at your best in any competition. Join our Competition Classes and push yourself to new heights, surrounded by teammates who share your dedication and passion for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.
